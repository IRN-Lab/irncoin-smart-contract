Roadmap – IRN Coin (2025–2027)

This roadmap outlines the strategic development phases of IRN Coin and the IRN ecosystem from 2025 through 2027.

2025 – Foundation & Infrastructure

• Launch of IRN‑Lab organization on GitHub

• Release of IRNCoin ERC‑20 smart contract on Polygon

• Deployment of main repository (irncoin-smart-contract)

• Initial documentation: overview, tokenomics, smart contract, security

• Release of public ABI for developers

• Setup of development branch (dev) for future upgrades

• Website preparation for IRN ecosystem (irnlab.ir)

2026 – Expansion Phase

• Launch of IRN dashboard / DApp for tracking balances and transfers

• Integration with wallets and third‑party explorers

• Submission to token listing platforms and aggregators

• Marketing and community-building efforts (X, Telegram, website)

• Beta release of ecosystem tools under IRN‑Lab GitHub

• Partnerships with small-scale blockchain projects

2027 – Ecosystem Growth

• Launch of IRN utilities and ecosystem services

• Development of staking‑related tools (non‑custodial)

• Integration with Web3 apps and cross‑platform services

• Property expansion: IRN‑Lab multi‑repo ecosystem

• Long-term compliance, documentation, and stability improvements

Long-Term Vision

IRN Coin aims to build a secure, transparent, and decentralized ecosystem under the IRN‑Lab brand, enabling long-term Web3 utility and community-driven growth.
