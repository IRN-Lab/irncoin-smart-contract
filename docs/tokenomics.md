Tokenomics – IRN Coin (IRN)

This document provides the official tokenomics of IRN Coin deployed on the Polygon PoS network.

Token Information

• Name: IRN Coin

• Symbol: IRN

• Standard: ERC‑20

• Network: Polygon Proof‑of‑Stake (PoS)

• Total Fixed Supply: 255,000,000 IRN

• Decimals: 18

• Contract Version: v1.0.0 (Initial Release)

Supply Model

IRN Coin uses a fixed total supply model. No minting function is available to increase total supply beyond 255 million tokens.

Distribution Model

Distribution of IRN Coin tokens is planned through:

• Ecosystem development

• Community rewards

• Partnerships

• Liquidity provisioning

• Long‑term protocol growth

Detailed allocation percentages will be published in future releases.

Burn Policy

While no automatic burn mechanism exists in the contract, manual burn events may be executed in future updates or governance decisions.

Utility Overview

IRN Coin is designed to serve multiple functions within the ecosystem:

• On‑chain payments

• Exchange trading

• Membership and authentication

• Integration with future IRN‑Lab services

• Token‑based governance (planned)

Roadmap Connection

Tokenomics is aligned with the IRN Coin 2025‑2027 Roadmap to ensure gradual and sustainable ecosystem expansion.
